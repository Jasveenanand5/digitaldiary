How Your Money Travels

When you make a payment or check your balance, your request starts a journey. Here's how it happens:

  • Your Phone: Your phone sends the transaction request securely.
  • The Bank: The bank stores your funds and awaits instructions.
  • The API: This acts as a bridge, translating your request into commands the bank understands.
  • The Merchant: Once authorised, the merchant receives the funds, completing your transaction.

All this happens in just seconds, ensuring your transactions are seamless and secure.
